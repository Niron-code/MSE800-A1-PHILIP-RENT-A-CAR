static book_rental_menu(user, texts)¶

Allows a customer to book a rental car by selecting dates and a car. Validates date input and displays available cars for the selected period. Calculates total fee and confirms booking with the user.

Displays the customer booking menu for managing bookings. Allows viewing, cancelling, and updating bookings for the logged-in customer.
